All That Glitters

A Fantasy Renaissance Tabletop Role-Playing Game

Table of Contents

All That Glitters is a fantasy tabletop role-playing game set in a world inspired by the late medieval to early renaissance period. There's fantasy races like dwarves and elves, knights in shining armor, some gunpowder, and plenty of magic. The central theme of the game is the call of adventure, where souls of every stripe leave behind the comfortable and familiar in order to bravely explore the unknown, fight evil, or find their fortune—often all three.

In terms of gameplay, the game has three goals:

  1. To increase the importance of loot.
  2. To provide guidelines for creating balanced homebrew content
  3. To simplify combat, making it faster and more accessible.
  4. To increase flexibility when leveling characters, without sacrificing variety.
