Throughout the handbook, several abbreviations are used to keep the text reasonably concise. Most of these are explained in more detail in chapter 3. These are the most common:

Combat:

  • HP: Health Points
  • WP: Willpower
  • BC: Block Chance
  • DC: Dodge Chance
  • MIT: Mitigation (Similar to Armor Class)
  • CT: Critical Threshold

Note: DC can easily be confused with difficulty class, which is used in other games and often abbreviated DC. Difficulty class is not used in this game, but expresses the same concept as difficulty threshold (DT).

Attributes:

  • STR: Strength
  • SPD: Speed
  • DEX: Dexterity
  • INT: Intelligence
  • CHA: Charisma
  • WILL: Willpower

Other:

  • DM: Dungeon Master
  • DT: Difficulty Threshold
  • NPC: Non-player Character
