Throughout the handbook, several abbreviations are used to keep the text reasonably concise. Most of these are explained in more detail in chapter 3. These are the most common:

Combat:

  • HP: Health Points (Used to determine when characters fall unconscious from their wounds.)
  • WP: Willpower (Expended when using powerful abilities.)
  • BC: Block Chance (The chance that an attack is mitigated by armor or shields.)
  • DC: Dodge Chance (The chance that an attack misses a character entirely.)
  • MIT: Mitigation (Determines how likely an attack against a character is to be effective. Similar to Armor Class.)
  • CT: Critical Threshold (The value on the d20 required to roll a critical. Starts at 20.)

Note: DC can easily be confused with difficulty class, which is used in other games and often abbreviated DC. Difficulty Class is called "Difficulty Threshold" in All That Glitters.

Attributes:

  • STR: Strength
  • SPD: Speed
  • DEX: Dexterity
  • INT: Intelligence
  • CHA: Charisma
  • WILL: Willpower

Other:

  • DM: Dungeon Master
  • DT: Difficulty Threshold
  • NPC: Non-player Character
