Now that you know the basics, you're ready to create a character. This section of the text will refer to some mechanics which aren't explained until later sections. If that bothers you, you can skip ahead to later chapters and revisit this section later. However, you don't need to perfectly understand the rules to make an excellent character. I recommend at least a quick read of this chapter before moving on.

Creating a character in All That Glitters is really simple. Just follow these steps:

  1. Choose a Race
  2. Choose a Class
  3. Choose an Alignment
  4. Write a Backstory
  5. Choose starting Equipment
  6. Fill out your Character Sheet
