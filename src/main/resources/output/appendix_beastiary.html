The Planes of Existence

Broadly speaking, the world consists of physical, astral, infernal, and ethereal planes.

The physical planes are distinguished by strict natural laws and a magical veil which prevents creatures therein from obtaining more than a glimpse of the subtle mysteries of nature. Spirits within any of the physical planes are called mortals, or lesser spirits.

The astral planes are any of various planes which are "softer" and more responsive to conscious intent than the physical. The lower astral mirrors the physical world, and contains low vibrational entities such as lurkers, energivores, and egregores, low ranking demons which prey on sentient energy. Higher astral planes are dreamlike, vibrant, and surreal, and are occupied by higher beings such as fey, djinn, dragons, and angels.

The infernal planes are hells, created by demons. Each hell is simultaneously a prison and a palace for its reigning archdemon, alongside various lesser devils and enslaved spirits. Hells are filled with oppressive and joyless energy, and tend to suffocate emotions such as hope and compassion the longer one spends there. Hells are virtually indistinguishable from the physical plane, but are actually extremely condensed astral planes that are resistant to change from conscious intent.

The ethereal planes are timeless planes of pure thought. They are changeless and eternal, and cannot be fully entered by mortal spirits. Each ethereal plane is the highest and purest expression of the god which inhabits it. Little is known about these planes, except that they act as sources of archetypical energy such as pure flame, pure light, or various pure emotions.

The Spirits of the Universe

In general, beings are divided into three classes: mortals, higher beings, and gods.

Mortals (animus mortalis) are inhabitants of the physical plane, and are expressed via a physical body. Mortals tend to increase in energy over time, eventually becoming higher beings after thousands or millions of years. On death, mortal spirits wander the lower astral until they either reincarnate or are summoned into the domain of a god or demon.

Higher beings (animus aethera, or demigods) are inhabitants of the nonphysical (astral) planes. Roughly speaking, there are four types of higher beings: demons, dragons, fey (including djinn) and angels. These beings serve to create, preserve, and destroy aspects of the physical realm, according to their own whims or the will of their creator. They can enter the physical world at will, but must expend energy to manifest a physical body to do so. If the body is destroyed, they return to their home in the astral and recuperate until eventually enough energy is gained for them to return. This process usually takes around 30 days to a year.

Gods (animus celestis) are eternal beings of infinite creative power. Their existence is beyond human concepts such as pain, fear, death, or ambition. They can communicate with mortals and demigods, usually through exceptionally pure spirits called angels, but can do so directly if they wish to do so.

There are five types of beings which can assist or oppose players: Beasts, Invertebrates, Constructs, Undead, and Demigods.

Amorphous: Oozes, and other formless entities.

Plants: Photosynthetic entities possessing biological and morphological similarities to plants.

Constructs

Conglomerations of non-living components animated by lesser spirits.

Undead

Death-touched entities either returned to unnatural semblance of life, or undeparted spirits of the deceased. These entities are greatly harmed by weapons of silver.

Vampiric: Entities sustained by the lifeblood of living, preferably sentient beasts.

Zomboid: Re-animated beasts, humanoids, and ogroids.

Spectrals: Ghosts and other non-departed spirits.

Demigods

Pure spiritual beings which may manifest in the material plane. They never die, but may be banished to their home realm.

Demonic: Cursed spirits from the hells, whose power cannot alleviate their suffering.

Fey: Ancient spirits of nature and the wilds, who cannot be tamed.

Angelic: Joyful spirits who choose to serve the divine Source Consciousness.

Draconic: Ferocious beings of absolute power. They may take any form, but prefer that of the dragon.
