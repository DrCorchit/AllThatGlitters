School of Alchemy

Brew Aqua Regia

Academic toxin

Effect: The alchemical ritual renders of an extremely volatile acid that even dissolves inert metals such as gold and platinum. The reaction yields four ounces of liquid, which are stored in a stoppered flask. When thrown at a target after the incantation has been uttered, it deals 4d4 acid damage and reduces armor by 1.

Note: The mixture remains stable and can be poured from one container to another until the correct incantation is uttered. Thereupon, the mixture explodes into a violent orange-colored froth and the pressure builds until it escapes or shatters the flask. If the incantation is never uttered, the mixture decomposes and becomes inert after a long rest.

Training Requirements:

  • Intelligence: 16
  • Training Slots: 4

Casting Requirements:

  • Casting Time: 1 hour
  • Evocation AP: 4
  • Location: Alchemical Laboratory
  • Materials: 1 ounce of nitric acid and 3 ounces of brimstone.

Scaling:

  • Damage: Improved chemical purity increases damage by 1d4 for every two points of intelligence above 16.
Brew Curative

Common potion

Effect: A crimson philter of lion or bear's blood is precipitated in alchemical solution. The reaction yields 4 ounces of liquid, which are stored in a stoppered flask. As an action, you may drink up to 4 ounces from the flask, healing 1d4 damage each.

Note: If the potion is not consumed, the mixture denatures and becomes inert after a long rest.

Training Requirements:

  • Intelligence: 14
  • Training Slots: 3

Casting Requirements:

  • Casting Time: 1 action
  • Evocation AP: 6
  • Location: Alchemical Laboratory
  • Materials: 1 ounce of fresh blood, taken from a lion or bear.

Scaling:

  • Quantity: Improved distillation procedures allow you to produce one additional ounce of curative for each point of intelligence above 14.
Brew Neurotoxin

Common toxin

Effect: A potent concentrate of serpent venom is precipitated in alchemical solution. The reaction yields 1 ounce of liquid, which are stored in a vial. With an action, you can coat a weapon or up to ten arrows with the solution. Any target damaged by these weapons must pass a DT 12 willpower saving throw or become paralysed.

Note: If the vial is not used, the mixture decomposes and becomes inert after a long rest.

Training Requirements:

  • Intelligence: 14
  • Training Slots: 3

Casting Requirements:

  • Casting Time: 30 minutes
  • Evocation AP: 3
  • Location: Alchemical Laboratory
  • Materials: 4 viper fangs

Scaling:

  • Potency: Saving throw DT increases by 1 for every point of intelligence above 14.

School of Astrology

Astral Gate

Esoteric planetary astrological ceremony

Effect: Two parties working in tandem perform corresponding ceremonies in two desigated locations. After confirming appropriate astrological alignment, the two groups (typically working via psychic connection) gather around a circle and perform a complex ceremony involving specific incantations. When the ceremony is complete, a permanent astral gate appears in that location, linked to the corresponding gate constructed by the cooperating party. The portal remains open indefinitely, unless another ceremony is convened to banish it.

Note: The AP cost of the spell is paid twice, once by each party.

Training Requirements:

  • Intelligence: 20
  • Willpower: 16
  • Training Slots: 3

Casting Requirements:

  • Casting Time: 1 day
  • Evocation AP: 200

Mind Control

Forbidden telepathic concentration spell

Target: One creature within 100 feet feet

Effect: The caster takes full control of being they can see within range. You automatically gain the psychic sight and major telepathy. The target makes a DT 10 perception check to realize they are being psychically dominated. If the target succeeds, they may make an additional DT 18 willpower check to resist your control. If they succeed this check, the spell ends and they have advantage on similar checks against you. If it fails, they may make additional attempts to resist at the end of each of their combat turns, or before making actions significantly against their interests. Additionally, if the target is aware that they are being dominated, they may give subtle tells such as speaking with unnatural vocal inflections or eye twitches.

Training Requirements:

  • Intelligence: 14
  • Charisma: 14
  • Willpower: 18
  • Training Slots: 6
  • Spells: Major Telepathy, Psychic Sight

Casting Requirements:

  • Casting Time: 2 actions
  • Evocation AP: 10
  • Concentration AP: 2

Scaling:

  • Range: Additional 50 feet of range per point of willpower above 18.
  • Difficulty: The difficulty of recognition and resistance checks are modified by the difference between the caster and target's combined intelligence and charisma scores. The DT can decrease if the target is more intelligent or charismatic than the caster.
