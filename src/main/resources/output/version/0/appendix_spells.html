School of Alchemy

Brew: Acumen Philter

Academic potion

Effect: Produces a cerulean solution of crushed pearl and gold dust, which forms at the top of an alchemical mixture. The reaction yields 1 ounce of product, which is stored in a vial. As an action, you may drink the contents of the vial, gaining 2 points of intelligence until the next long rest.

Note: Consuming this potion has no effect if one's intelligence has already been increased by another potion.

Training Requirements:

    Intelligence: 16 Training Slots: 3

Casting Requirements:

    1 hour Evocation Willpower: 6 Location: Alchemical Laboratory Materials: A pearl and a pile of gold dust, plus other ingredients costing 50 gold.

Modifiers:

  • Potency: Bonus intelligence is increased by an additional point for every 3 points of intelligence above 16 when the potion was brewed.

Brew: Aqua Regia

Academic toxin

Effect: The alchemical ritual renders an extremely volatile acid that even dissolves inert metals such as gold and platinum. The reaction yields four ounces of liquid, which are stored in a stoppered flask. When thrown at a target after the incantation has been uttered, it deals 4d6 acid damage and reduces armor by 3.

Note: The mixture remains stable and can be poured from one container to another until the correct incantation is uttered. Thereupon, the mixture explodes into an orange-colored froth and the pressure builds until it escapes or shatters the flask.

Training Requirements:

    Intelligence: 16 Training Slots: 4

Casting Requirements:

    1 hour Evocation Willpower: 10 Location: Alchemical Laboratory Materials: 1 ounce of nitric acid and 3 ounces of brimstone, plus additional materials costing 80 gold.

Modifiers:

  • Location: If brewed using field equipment rather than an alchemical laboratory, the caster must pass a DT 12 int saving throw or the spell fails and AP and ingredients are lost.
  • maintenance: If the incantation is never uttered, the mixture eventually becomes volatile and spews from its flask. This mishap can be forestalled indefinitely via a daily ritual which takes 20 minutes and expends 10 AP.
  • Damage: Improved chemical purity increases damage by 1d6 for every two points of intelligence above 16.

The alternate version takes effect after a week's time, but its effects may be forestalled or dismissed by an appropriate incantation. This incantation is known only to the brewer of the potion unless it is disclosed to others or overheard during preparation.

School of Astrology

Astral Gate

Esoteric planetary astrological ceremony

Effect: Two parties working in tandem perform corresponding ceremonies in two desigated locations. After confirming appropriate astrological alignment, the two groups (typically working via psychic connection) gather around a circle and perform a complex ceremony involving specific incantations. When the ceremony is complete, a permanent astral gate appears in that location, linked to the corresponding gate constructed by the cooperating party. The portal remains open indefinitely, unless another ceremony is convened to banish it.

Note: The AP cost of the spell is paid twice, once by each party.

Training Requirements:

    Intelligence: 20 Charisma: 16 Training Slots: 3

Casting Requirements:

    1 day Evocation Willpower: 200

Sweet Dreams

Esoteric wyrd ceremony

Target: All allies within 15 feet feet

Duration: 8 hours

Effect: You and all participants call out to the fey, thanking them for their role in maintaining the balance of nature and protecting the life force. You invite them to visit you in your dreams, calling for succor, entertainment, and guidance. All participants roll a 1d4, and gain an effect accordingly:

  1. Hellish Nightmares: Tormented by cruel fey, no rest can be had. No AP is gained from the long rest.
  2. Vivid Visions: Playful and adventurous fey take the dreamer on wondrous adventures seeming to last for many days. Upon awakening, the individual gains 1d4 temporary AP.
  3. Quiet Quandaries: The dreamer awakens beside a pool of living water, which idly asks the dreamer a question. After it has been answered, it invites the dreamer to ask a question of their own. If the adventurer answered the water's question truthfully, it responds in kind. Gain 1d6 temporary AP.
  4. Allmother's Embrace: The dreamers is escorted by powerful fey to the magical heart of the forest. They are greeted by a gentle yet powerful dryad called the Allmother, who embraces them tenderly. Gain 1d8 temporary HP and AP.

Training Requirements:

    Instinct: 15 Training Slots: 4

Casting Requirements:

    30 minutes Evocation Willpower: 40 Location: The ceremony can only be performed within a forest or jungle.
