  • Versatile

Small Weapons

Small weapons are those which are easily wielded in one hand, including everyday tools improvised as weapons. They require no especial skill to use, are easily concealed, and are typically wielded by civilians. These weapons automatically have the light property, and can be dual-wielded or used in the offhand.
