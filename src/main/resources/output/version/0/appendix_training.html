Here is a list of training available to players:

Attribute Training: Elite

Cost: 50 Gold and 1 training slot.
Effect: The player hires an experienced coach or tutor to assist them in breaking their limits. Increases the soft cap for an attribute by 2. Purchasable once per attribute.

Attribute Training: Olympian

Cost: 100 Gold and 2 training slots.

Prerequisite: The player has already achieved “Attribute Training: Elite” for this attribute.

Effect: The player increases the soft cap for this attribute by 2, up to the hard cap. Purchasable once per attribute.

Proficiency Training: Remediation

Cost: 50 Gold and 5 training slots

Effect: The player performs a DT 10 skill check in the chosen skill. If the check succeeds, skill recovers from “deficient” to “normal”. Otherwise, the skill remains at “deficient” but training slots are restored.

Proficiency Training: Apprenticeship

Cost: 20 Gold and 4 training slots

Effect: The player performs a DT 12 skill check in the chosen skill. If the check succeeds, skill advances from “normal” to “proficient”. Otherwise, the skill remains at “deficient” but training slots are restored.

Proficiency Training: Expertise

Cost: 50 gold and 5 training slots.

Effect: The player performs a DT 15 skill check in the chosen skill. If the check succeeds, skill advances from “proficient” to “expert”. Otherwise, the skill remains at “proficient” but training slots are restored.

Proficiency Training: Mastery

Cost: 100 Gold and 6 training slots

Effect: The player performs a DT 18 skill check in the chosen skill. If the check succeeds, skill advances from “expert” to “master”. Otherwise, the skill remains at “expert” but training slots are restored.

Combat Training: Mordhau Grip

Cost: 50 Gold and 3 training slots

Effect: The player unlocks the Mordhau Grip ability for large swords with straight crossguards. The player may use the “change grip” action to activate mordhau grip, dealing bludgeoning damage with these swords.

Combat Training: Estoc

Cost: 75 Gold and 3 training slots

Effect: The player unlocks the Piercing Strikeability for the estoc, reducing BC by half for opponents in any type of mail.

Note: The Estoc is an unbladed sword specifically designed for piercing chainmail. Due to its lack of a blade, the sword is not ideal as a civilian self-defense weapon.

Combat Training: Shield Bash

Cost 50 gold and 2 training slots

Effect: The player masters the ability Shield Bash, allowing extra damage with bucklers and shields.

Combat Training: Shield Bash

Combat Training: Shield Charge

Cost: 50 Gold, 1 training slot.

Effect: The player acquires the Shield Chargeability. While carrying a greatshield or mantlet, the player may expend 2 willpower

Combat Training: Polearm Charge

Cost: 50 gold, 1 training slot.

Effect: The player acquires the Polearm Chargeability. While carrying a pole arm, the player may expend 1 willpower to charge at the enemy, changing the damage type of the polearm weapon from STR to SPD.

Combat Training: Flailing Strike

Cost: 75 Gold and 3 training slots

Effect: The player learns to use the flail, an unusual weapon designed to bypass shields. The player unlocks the Flailing Strikeability for the flail, reducing BC gained by opponents with shields. Specifically, the shield no longer provides any BC unless it is actively blocking, in which case BC provided is reduced by half.

Combat Training: Hook Sword

Cost: 100 Gold and 4 training slots

Effect: The player learns to use the hook sword, a rare sword designed to trip up or disarm opponents. When successfully parrying, the player may disarm the opponent bypassing a martial arts skill check. The DT of the skill check is equal to the opponent’s dexterity attribute.

Combat Training: Grappling Hook

Cost: 100 Gold and 6 training slots

Prerequisite: 15 dexterity.

Effect: The player learns to use the grappling hook in combat, acquiring the ability Come Hither. Using an offhand action, the player may make throw the grappling hook at a target within 20 feet. Upon a successful accuracy skill check against the target’s dexterity, the target is pulled 15 feet towards the player.

Mobility Training: Grappling Hook

Cost: 50 gold and 1 training slots

Effect: The player learns to use the grappling hook to escape, acquiring the ability Vertical Escape. Using a main hand action, the player can throw the grappling hook to any point overhead in a 20 foot radius and climb using their movement points.
