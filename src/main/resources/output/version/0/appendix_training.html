Athletics Training

Falling

Training Slots: 4

The player practices landing on various surfaces from various heights, using a rolling technique whereby weight is distributed between the feet, legs, buttocks, and shoulders. Having learned various falling techniques, fall damage is reduced by 10 + 1d10.

Freestyle

Training Slots: 3

The player spends one week practicing freestyle swimming. Swim speed is increased by 50%.

Leaping and Landing

Training Slots: 3

The player spends one week practicing jumping from one surface to another, landing on a variety of terrain types. Jump height and distance are increased by 50%. The player gains a 1d4 bonus when making saving throws to avoid landing prone.

Lung Capacity

Training Slots: 4

The player spends one week practicing holding their breath and diving under water. After persistent practice, the player learns to hold their breath for 50% longer than normal.

Vertical Escape

Training Slots: 3 Gold: 20

The player learns to use the grappling hook to make a hasty escape. If surroundings are appropriate, the player can throw the hook up to 30 feet vertically as an action. On a successful DT 12 Mobility check, the grappling hook latches. The player can use an action to climb 10 feet up the rope, away from enemy melee attacks.

Wall Run

Training Slots: 4

The player learns to run a short distance along or up walls, and to leap backwards off of them.

Combat Training

Cleaving Strike

Training Slots: 3

Before making a slashing weapon attack, the player may spend 3 willpower to extend the weapon attack to an additional creature. The attacker makes separate attack rolls for both creatures; if both rolls are successful, the second attack does half damage. Both creatures must be within melee range.

Come Hither

Training Slots: 4 Gold: 30

The player learns to use the grappling hook in combat. Using an action, the player may throw the grappling hook at a target within 20 feet. Upon a successful Accuracy check against the target’s mitigation, the target is pulled 15 feet towards the player.

Defender

Training Slots: 6

The maximum DC penalty for wearing heavy armor is decreased by 2. The player no longer has disadvantage to mobility while wearing plate armor.

Estoc Master

Training Slots: 3 Gold: 30

The player learns the proper use of the estoc, a martial thrusting sword. When attacking with the estoc, opponents wearing chain mail gain half BC from their armor.

Feint Attack

Training Slots: 4

The player chooses a target and feigns an attack against it. The target's DC is reduced by 1d8 until the start of its next turn. If the target succeeds a dexterity saving throw equal to the attacker's dexterity score, their DC is only reduced by 1d4.

Flailing Strike

Training Slots: 4 Gold: 30

When making a weapon attack with a flail, the player may expend 1 willpower to ignore the bonus block chance provided by a shield. If the shield is actively blocking, its block chance is reduced by half instead.

Hook Sword Disarm

Training Slots: 4 Gold: 30

The player expends 1 willpower to make a special strike with the hook sword. The target must succeed a strength saving throw equal to the attacker's strength score or else their weapon is wrenched from their grasp.

Hook Sword Link Strike

Training Slots: 4 Gold: 30

If the player is dual wielding hook swords, they may expend 2 willpower to make a two-handed attack. The damage die for the attack is increased to 1d10 and the attack range is increased to 10 feet.

Keshik

Training Slots: 3 Gold: 30

The player learns to manipulate and aim a bow and arrow from horseback. Range attacks while mounted no longer have disadvantage.

Lancer

Training Slots: 3 Gold: 30

The player can use speed as the primary stat when attacking with polearms, and has advantage when making polearm attacks while mounted.

Mordhau

Training Slots: 4 Gold: 30

The player learns the proper use of the mordhau technique, and can deal bludgeoning damage with mordhau weapons.

Parry

Training Slots: 2 Gold: 30

The player may attempt to parry melee attacks using a reaction. When parrying, mitigation is increased by the parry amount of the weapon. Parrying an attack costs a reaction, regardless of whether the attack is successful.

Polearm Haft Strike

Training Slots: 2 Gold: 30

The player may expend an action to follow up a polearm attack with a haft strike. The player makes a normal melee attack roll. haft strike deals 1d4 damage, and does not reduce dodge chance.

Riposte

Training Slots: 4 Gold: 50

The player may expend 2 willpower to follow up a successful parry with a counterattack. The attack roll must use dexterity as the base stat.

Shield Bash

Training Slots: 4 Gold: 30

The player strikes directly with their shield. On a successful hit, the player deals 1d4 bludgeoning damage.

Proficiency Training

Apprenticeship

Training Slots: 3 Gold: 30

The player chooses a skill in which they are not proficient. On a successful DT 12 skill check, they attain proficiency. Training slots are only expended if the check is successful, but gold is not refunded.

Expertise

Training Slots: 4 Gold: 40

The player chooses a skill in which they are proficient. On a successful DT 16 skill check, their skill advances to expertise. Training slots are only expended if the check is successful, but gold is not refunded.

Mastery

Training Slots: 6 Gold: 60

The player chooses a skill in which they are an expert. On a successful DT 20 skill check, their skill advances to mastery. Training slots are only expended if the check is successful, but gold is not refunded.

Remediation

Training Slots: 4 Gold: 40

The player chooses a skill in which they are deficient. On a successful DT 10 skill check, they overcome the deficiency. Training slots are only expended if the check is successful, but gold is not refunded.
